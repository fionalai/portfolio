Project Goals

The goal of this project was to increase usability, understanding, and improve the overall aesthetic of the website. The business goal was to increase traffic and engagement, and decrease website drop-off rates.

Defining the Users

Furnishr‚Äôs target audience encompass both B2B and B2C customers. B2B customers include users who rent out their homes for profit like AirBnB Hosts and property / real estate investors. B2C customers are users who travel for work, such as expats and relocating workers.

Establishing Business Requirements

I conducted whiteboarding and card sorting workshops after gathering business requirements. These workshops allowed me to restructure the information architecture of the application and develop a new theme suitable for an interior design SaaS start-up.

Solution

My solution was three-fold:

  1. Create user-friendly content
    • Based on user surveys and data collected from the application, I discovered that Furnishr‚Äôs services and pricing were not communicated effectively and therefore confused our users. Additionally, I implemented simple descriptive language to reduce friction at every touch point.

  2. Target businesses directly

It was found that the previous site was primarily targeting B2C customers, which excluded the needs of B2B users.   3. Establish a new style guide
    • Furnishr‚Äôs design principles are innovative, clean, and simple. I elevated the designs by creating a theme around these design principles.

Conclusion

Dealing with large changes in a company's website requires deeper UX explorations to understand customer pain points when visiting the site, and moderated user testing to validate our designs before implementation. Ultimately, this is just one version of the website. Moving forward, multiple enhancements and user tests are necessary to ensure success.
